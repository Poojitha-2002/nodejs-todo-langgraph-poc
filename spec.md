# Login Page Specification

## Description
The Login Page allows registered users to access their accounts by entering their email and password. Successful login will grant access to the user's account, while failure cases typically involve incorrect email/password combinations.

## Input Fields
- **Email**: A field for users to enter their registered email address.
- **Password**: A field for users to enter their password.

## Validation Rules
- The email format must be valid (e.g., must contain "@" and a domain).
- The password must meet certain criteria, which may include:
  - Minimum length (e.g., at least 8 characters).
  - Complexity requirements (e.g., must include uppercase letters, lowercase letters, numbers, and special characters).
